Neumont Innovations Launch Pad

What is the Innovations Launch Pad?

The Innovations Launch Pad is an opportunity for students and graduates to develop and market their ideas. The student who had the idea owns that intellectual property, not Neumont.

The Launch Pad will assist in bringing together a team of students from not only Neumont, but the University of Utah, Brigham-Young University, and Westminster College to develop the student's idea into a real world product or service.

The innovation center will also help students develop prototypes; create a value proposition and business model; meet with industry professionals to assist in the development of ideas; establish connection with funding groups such as Zions Bank, VentureCapital.org, and others; and with getting involved in statewide business competitions.
